About VirtualConnect

The comprehensive digital platform connecting students, faculty, and administration at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology

Our Platform

Course Recommendations

Our intelligent system analyzes your academic performance to suggest the most suitable courses based on your strengths and interests, helping you make informed decisions about your educational path.

Student Support

The SSD Live Chat provides real-time assistance for academic and personal support, ensuring students have access to help whenever they need it.

Admin Portal

The Admin Portal is a secure platform designed for Limkokwing University staff to communicate directly with students through a dedicated chat interface. It ensures real-time support for academic and administrative inquiries for visual consistency across the university's digital ecosystem.
